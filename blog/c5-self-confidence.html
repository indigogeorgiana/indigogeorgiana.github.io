Searching inside yourself: The Unexpected Path to Achieving Success, Happiness(and World Peace)

Chapter 4 -Self-Awareness That Leads to Self-Confidence

What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

There is defintely I connnection, I could even say in some instances an overlap between emotional awareness, accurate self-assesment and self-confidence. All areas which every human has had to face at some point in their lives. The author of the book describes all three.."simple linear relationship." Linear to me means a series of steps on one long line of a process. Or a move from one stage into another or connected.

Try at least one of the self awareness practices from the chapter (Body Scan, Journaling). What was your experience of it? How did you feel?

This morning I pracitised the body scan practice and found it to be very beneficial. I found the technique was very good for focus as the book promised. I did have some problem trying to focus in the first few chapters of this book with focus and my internal voice. The exercise took all my attention I found myself repeating the process over in my head many times to remember in what order I needed to focus one. In all it was very enlightening.

What did you take away from the "My Emotions are Not Me" section?

That through mindfulness I can better control my emotions, and most defintely that my emotions are not who I am. However upon reflection, I find personally my emotions are apart of who I am and when critizied or struggle with something I tend to search internally more that outside of myself in times of doubt. This is not something I have always done, but something, I have learned how to best navigate myself through past relationships and friendships. Silence is sometimes golden, pause, think, then respond.
